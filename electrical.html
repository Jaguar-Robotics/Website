What We Do

Our Role.


The electrical subteam is responsible for every wire, connector, sensor, and controller on the robot. A clean, reliable electrical system is what separates a robot that performs consistently from one that fails at the worst moment. We design, wire, and maintain all electrical systems from the ground up.

Electrical reliability is critical in FRC. A single loose connector can end a match. We build our systems to withstand the full 2.5-minute match including high-G collisions, vibration, and rapid direction changes.
