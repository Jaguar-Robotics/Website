Team 7415 Jaguar Robotics is the FIRST Robotics Competition team of deToledo High School in West Hills, California. Every season, our students design, build, wire, and program a full-scale competition robot in just six weeks.

We compete across Southern California with a culture built on collaboration, rigor, and gracious professionalism. We are a proud member of Team Anvil — a SoCal FRC collaboration alliance.
