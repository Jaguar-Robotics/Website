What We Do

Our Role.


The outreach subteam connects Team 7415 to the broader community — running STEM events, mentoring younger students, managing our public presence, and spreading FIRST's mission beyond the competition field. Gracious professionalism isn't just something we practice at competitions.

Beyond competition, we are committed to FIRST's mission of inspiring young people in STEM. Every student on the team is an ambassador for gracious professionalism — on and off the field.
